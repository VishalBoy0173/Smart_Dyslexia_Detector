About Smart Dyslexia Detector

This tool uses AI (YOLOv11) to detect dyslexia indicators from children’s handwriting. It analyzes each letter and highlights Normal, Reversal, and Corrected patterns.

Features:

  • Upload scanned handwriting images
  • Write directly on an interactive canvas
  • Receive a risk score and per‑letter feedback
  • Practice games to strengthen writing skills

Built for educational screening. Not a medical diagnosis.
